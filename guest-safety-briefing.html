Lodge Safety & Logistics Briefing

Property Access: Check-in is at 3:00 PM. Access is via a unique 4-digit code provided 24 hours prior via email. The system is 100% contactless.

Connectivity: The property is serviced by high-speed Starlink Satellite (150-250 Mbps).

Safety Alert: The on-property lava tube and cave system are strictly closed to the public for safety assessments. Please do not attempt to locate or enter these areas.

National Park Logistics: Kīlauea conditions change daily. Visitors are advised that Park parking lots typically reach full capacity by 10:00 AM. E-Bikes are the recommended method for guaranteed caldera access.

Recovery Services: The Bio-Regeneration Suite (featuring OlyLife P90 and vibration plates) is available for guest recovery after hiking. Room 4 features a private whirlpool tub.

Dining: Nearby recommendations include Ohelo Café, The Rim, and Kilauea Lodge. Note: Lodge suites feature kitchenettes but do not include full stoves.
