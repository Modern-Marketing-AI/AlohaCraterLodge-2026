Rainy Day

It's a misty cloud-forest day — perfect for grabbing a book, enjoying local tea in the common area, or taking a drive down to Hilo, which is often sunny when it rains up here.

Stargazer

For zero light pollution, head into the National Park after dark. The Devastation Trail parking lot or the Kīlauea Overlook offer incredible views of the Milky Way.

Take a brisk morning walk through Volcano Village to the local Sunday Farmers Market at Cooper Center, or grab fresh coffee and pastries at the local cafes.
