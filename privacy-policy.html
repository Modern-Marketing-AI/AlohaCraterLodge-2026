Privacy Policy

How Aloha Crater Lodge collects, uses, and protects the information you share with us.

Section 01

Data Collection, Cookies & Analytics

Aloha Crater Lodge ("the Lodge," "we," "us") collects information you voluntarily provide when you contact us, submit an inquiry, or complete a reservation through our booking platform. This includes your name, email address, phone number, and travel dates.

We also automatically collect certain technical data when you visit our website, including:

  • IP address and general geographic region
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website or search query that brought you to our site

We use industry-standard analytics tools to understand how guests navigate our site so we can improve their experience. These tools may place small text files called cookies on your device. Cookies allow us to recognize repeat visitors, remember preferences, and measure the effectiveness of our content.

You may disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Disabling cookies may affect some functionality of our site but will not prevent you from accessing information or making a reservation.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Section 02

Third-Party Processing — Booking Engine

Reservations at Aloha Crater Lodge are processed through CloudBeds, a third-party property management and booking platform. When you click "Reserve Basecamp" or complete a booking, you will be directed to CloudBeds' secure reservation interface.

CloudBeds independently collects and processes the information required to complete your reservation, including:

  • Full name and contact details
  • Payment card information (processed under PCI-DSS compliance)
  • Arrival and departure dates and room preferences
  • Special requests or notes you provide during booking

Information processed through CloudBeds is governed by CloudBeds' Privacy Policy, which is available on their website. Aloha Crater Lodge receives reservation confirmation data and your contact information in order to prepare your arrival and deliver your pre-check-in briefing.

We may also use retargeting or pixel technologies provided by our booking platform to help us reach travelers who have expressed interest in a stay. These technologies operate in accordance with applicable privacy law and industry opt-out standards, including the Digital Advertising Alliance's opt-out tool.

Section 03

Communication Consent — SMS & Email

By providing your phone number or email address through our contact form, booking platform, or "Text Concierge" feature, you consent to receive communications from Aloha Crater Lodge related to your reservation and stay. These may include:

  • Reservation confirmation and pre-arrival briefing documents
  • Your secure contactless entry code (delivered via SMS approximately 24 hours before arrival)
  • Post-stay follow-up or satisfaction check-ins
  • Occasional Lodge updates, availability alerts, or promotional offers (only if you opt in)

SMS messaging: Message and data rates may apply. You may opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP to any Lodge message. For assistance, reply HELP.

Email communications: Marketing emails include an unsubscribe link. Transactional emails (reservation confirmations, entry codes) are required for service delivery and cannot be opted out of while an active reservation exists.

We do not share your contact information with third-party marketers. Your information is used exclusively to serve your reservation and to improve your experience as a Lodge guest.
