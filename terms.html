Terms of Service

Reservation policies, equipment waivers, and property guidelines for all guests of Aloha Crater Lodge.

Section 01

Reservation & Cancellation Policies

Booking & Confirmation

Reservations at Aloha Crater Lodge are confirmed upon receipt of a valid payment method through our third-party booking platform, Little Hotelier. A reservation confirmation number and pre-arrival briefing will be delivered to the email address provided at booking. Your secure entry code will be transmitted via SMS approximately 24 hours prior to your scheduled arrival.

Cancellation Policy

Standard Rate: Cancellations made more than 7 days prior to arrival are eligible for a full refund minus any applicable processing fees charged by our booking platform. Cancellations within 7 days of arrival are non-refundable.

Cancellation requests must be submitted directly through the booking confirmation portal or by contacting the Lodge via text or phone at (808) 345-4449. Email cancellation requests are accepted but may require 1 business day to process.

Modifications

Date modifications are permitted subject to availability and must be requested at least 48 hours prior to the original arrival date. The Lodge will make every effort to accommodate requested changes. Rate differences resulting from date changes will be applied at the time of modification.

No-Shows

Guests who fail to arrive on their confirmed arrival date without prior cancellation will forfeit the full reservation amount. No refunds will be issued for early departures after check-in has been completed.

Force Majeure

In the event of natural disasters, volcanic activity, mandatory government evacuation orders, or other circumstances beyond the Lodge's control that render the property inaccessible, affected reservations will receive a full refund or complimentary rebooking credit at the guest's discretion.

E-bike reservations must be made in advance through the post-booking Guest Portal. Fleet availability is limited and subject to maintenance windows. Refunds for unused e-bike time are not provided except in cases of mechanical failure confirmed by Lodge staff.

Section 03

Property Safety

General Conduct

Aloha Crater Lodge is a private property situated within a sensitive rainforest ecosystem. All guests are expected to conduct themselves in a manner that respects the property, its natural surroundings, and fellow guests. Quiet hours are observed from 10:00 PM to 7:00 AM.

Grounds & Natural Areas

Guests must adhere to all posted signage on the property at all times. Entry into unmarked natural areas, areas marked with restricted access signage, or any off-trail terrain on or adjacent to the property is strictly prohibited.

The Lodge grounds include natural vegetation, uneven terrain, and active rainforest areas that may present physical hazards in low-light or wet conditions. Guests are advised to use designated pathways between structures and to exercise appropriate caution when moving throughout the property after dark.

Volcanic & Environmental Awareness

Aloha Crater Lodge is located approximately 2 miles from Hawaii Volcanoes National Park. While the property is in a designated safe zone, guests should be aware of the following:

  • Volcanic gas (SO2) and vog levels near the crater rim may vary — always consult the USGS Hawaii Volcano Observatory daily reports before approaching the summit area
  • In the event of a volcanic eruption escalation or official evacuation order, guests must comply immediately with all instructions from Lodge staff and Hawaii County Civil Defense
  • Trail and road conditions in the national park can change rapidly — always obtain current park conditions from National Park Service rangers before deployment

Wildlife & Ecosystem

The Lodge grounds are home to endemic Hawaiian wildlife including nēnē (Hawaiian geese) and native bird species protected under state and federal law. Guests must not feed, approach, or disturb wildlife at any time. Introduction of non-native plants or animals to the property is strictly prohibited.

No Smoking Policy

Smoking, vaping, and open flames of any kind are prohibited in all indoor areas of the Lodge and within 25 feet of any structure. Violation of this policy will result in immediate termination of the reservation without refund and may result in assessment of cleaning fees.

Pet Policy

Aloha Crater Lodge is a pet-free property. Unauthorized pets on the premises will result in additional cleaning fees and may require early departure at the Lodge's discretion.
